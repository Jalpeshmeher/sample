Aqua Beach Farmhouse at Kalamb Beach

Located just a short drive from Nallasopara (6 km) and Virar (11 km), Aqua Beach Farmhouse offers a serene escape for couples, families, and friends. Nestled amidst picturesque farms and beautiful beaches, this charming farmhouse promises an unforgettable experience at an affordable price.

Key Features:
Stunning Sea-View Rooms:

The farmhouse boasts spacious rooms with large, sea-facing windows, providing guests with panoramic views of the beach and surrounding nature, making every moment relaxing and memorable.

Pet-Friendly:

Bring along your furry friends! Aqua Beach Farmhouse is a pet-friendly haven, allowing you to enjoy quality time with your pets while relaxing by the beach.

Evening Bonfires & Barbecues:

As night falls, gather around a cozy bonfire with your loved ones. Enjoy sizzling barbecue treats under the stars and create lasting memories in the tranquil beachside setting.

Perfect for All:

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat, a romantic getaway, or an adventurous family trip, Aqua Beach Farmhouse caters to all kinds of guests. Its beautiful surroundings and beach activities are ideal for everyone.

Adventure and Activities:

Dive into fun with exciting water sports, including jet skiing, banana boat rides, and speed boat rides. Explore the beach on horseback or camel rides, and experience the thrill of track beach car rides in the evening. There's no shortage of fun!

Natural Surroundings:

The farmhouse is surrounded by lush greenery and farms, offering a peaceful and picturesque environment. The soothing sound of waves and the beauty of nature create the perfect backdrop for relaxation.

Whether you want to enjoy an action-packed day with water sports or relax by the bonfire with a drink in hand, Aqua Beach Farmhouse at Kalamb Beach offers the ideal getaway for all kinds of travelers.
